About Me

Hi, I am Abira. Welcome to my academic webpage. I am a student of Doctoral of Philosophy (PhD) at the Department of Information Science, University of Otago, NZ. My current research work is related to moderating the expected behaviour of agents in open societies.

Research Interests

My primary research focus is in Computer Vision and Machine Learning and my current project is on deep learning based face recognition for biometrics. However, I am always interested in exploring other areas of computer vision and upcoming machine learning methods. At present I am looking to begin doctoral studies on using generative adversarial networks to synthesize photo-realistic images of mugshots from sketches and audio/text description inputs.
